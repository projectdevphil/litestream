About Litestream

Litestream is a free IPTV streaming service dedicated to providing Filipinos accessible entertainment, news, and media from anywhere in the world. We aim to bridge the gap between viewers and content by aggregating publicly available live TV channels into one simple, user-friendly interface.

Whether you are at home or abroad, Litestream ensures you stay connected with your favorite local programs without the need for expensive subscriptions or complex setups.

Disclaimer

Litestream acts solely as a search engine and aggregator for publicly available streaming URLs. We do not host, upload, or maintain any video files, streams, or media content on our servers.
