Channel Advisory IMPORTANT

Posted: December 2, 2025

MTV Live Cessation of Broadcast

MTV Live channel will permanently cease all broadcast transmission worldwide (excluding the U.S. feed) effective January 1, 2026.

The final day of programming for MTV Live will be Tuesday, December 31, 2025. Following the closure, the channel will be removed from the channel list starting on January 1, 2026.

This decision by Paramount Global reflects a global strategy shift in response to the changing landscape of music consumption.

You can continue to watch other great music programming on these channels:

MYX VEVO POP MTV LIVE (US VERSION)
